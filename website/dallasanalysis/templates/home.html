Introduction

Welcome to the Dallas Restaurant Analysis project. This project aims to provide insights into restaurant safety, helping residents make informed dining choices based on data-driven analyses of inspection scores and common health violations.

Objectives

  • Analyze inspection score trends to track improvements or declines over time.
  • Identify common and critical violations affecting restaurant scores.
  • Map violation hotspots to highlight areas requiring more oversight.
  • Provide actionable insights for restaurant owners and health officials.

Our Approach

Our approach includes analyzing inspection data, identifying high-risk areas, categorizing restaurants by type, and employing Natural Language Processing (NLP) to classify restaurants. By combining data analytics with geographic mapping, we create a comprehensive overview of restaurant safety in Dallas.
